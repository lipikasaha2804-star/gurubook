1
Who We Are

GuruBook is a fee and batch management application designed for coaching centres, tuition classes, and educational institutes in India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app. By using GuruBook, you agree to the practices described here.

2
What Information We Collect
InformationWhy We Collect It
Mobile phone numberTo verify your identity via OTP and send important updates
Centre / Institute nameTo identify your account and personalise your experience
Device IDTo link your account securely to your device
Payment status & datesTo manage your subscription to the app
Student fee recordsStored locally on your device only — we cannot see this

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any student personal information.

3
How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for:

  • ✓
    Verifying your identity when you register (OTP via phone call)
  • ✓
    Managing your app subscription and payment status
  • ✓
    Sending you important account notifications via WhatsApp
  • ✓
    Providing customer support when you contact us

We never use your data for advertising or share it with marketing companies.

4
Third-Party Services We Use

To run the app, we use these trusted services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — Securely stores your account data (India region). Privacy Policy
  • 2Factor.in — Delivers your OTP via phone call for login verification. Privacy Policy
  • EmailJS — Sends registration confirmation emails. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — Protects our app and secures API communications. Privacy Policy
5
Data Storage & Security

Your account data (phone number, centre name, device ID, payment status) is stored on Google Firebase servers, which are protected by Google's industry-leading security.

Your student fee records (student names, fees, attendance) are stored only on your own device. We never upload or see this data.

We retain your account data for as long as you have an active subscription. If you stop using the app, your data is automatically removed after 12 months of inactivity.

6
Your Rights

Under Indian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • ✓
    Access — Ask us what data we hold about you
  • ✓
    Correct — Ask us to fix incorrect information
  • ✓
    Delete — Ask us to remove your data from our systems
  • ✓
    Withdraw consent — Stop using the app at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on WhatsApp. We will respond within 7 working days.

7
Children's Privacy

GuruBook is designed for use by institute owners and teachers — adults only. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 18.

Student data entered into the app (names, fees) is managed entirely by the institute owner and stored only on their device. We have no access to this information.

8
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If there are significant changes, we will notify you via WhatsApp. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We are happy to help. Contact us on WhatsApp and we will respond personally within minutes.
